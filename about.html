Our Story

Discover how FindSab is transforming the way communities connect with local businesses and professionals.

About FindSab

FindSab was born from a simple idea: making it easier for people to discover and connect with the best local businesses and professionals in their community. Our name "FindSab" combines "Find" with "Sab" (meaning "all" in Hindi), reflecting our mission to help you find all the services you need, right in your neighborhood.

Our Mission

At FindSab, our mission is to strengthen local communities by creating meaningful connections between residents and the businesses that serve them. We believe that when local businesses thrive, communities prosper.

We're dedicated to providing a platform that makes it simple for users to discover, evaluate, and connect with trusted local professionals and businesses across Australia. Whether you're looking for a plumber, a caf√©, a doctor, or a yoga instructor, FindSab helps you make informed decisions about the services you need.

Our Vision

We envision a future where every community has a vibrant local economy, where small businesses can compete effectively with larger corporations, and where residents can easily find quality services without having to rely on impersonal chains or distant providers.

What Makes Us Different

Unlike other directories, FindSab focuses on building genuine community connections. We highlight the people behind the businesses, their stories, and their expertise. We believe that business is personal, especially at the local level.

Our platform is designed to be comprehensive yet easy to use, with powerful search tools that help you find exactly what you're looking for. We verify business information for accuracy and collect authentic reviews from real customers to help you make confident decisions.

Our Journey

The story of how FindSab has grown from an idea to Australia's trusted local business directory.

2018

The Beginning

FindSab started as a simple idea when our founder struggled to find reliable local services after moving to a new neighborhood. Recognizing this common challenge, the concept for a community-focused directory was born.

2019

Launch in Sydney

We launched our first version of FindSab in Sydney, focusing on connecting residents with local professionals and businesses in key neighborhoods.

2020

Expansion & Pandemic Response

As the pandemic hit, we quickly adapted our platform to help local businesses showcase their online services and delivery options, becoming a vital resource during lockdowns.

2021

National Growth

FindSab expanded to all major Australian cities, with thousands of businesses joining our platform and a growing user base across the country.

2022

Platform Enhancement

We launched our redesigned platform with advanced search features, verified reviews, and improved business profiles to better serve our growing community.

2023

Today & Beyond

Today, FindSab connects millions of Australians with over 5,000 local businesses across the country. We continue to innovate and expand our services to better serve communities nationwide.
